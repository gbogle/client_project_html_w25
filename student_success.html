Student Success at UMSI

Health and Wellness

UMSI recognizes the importance of physical and mental well-being. Students have access to a variety of health services that cater to both their physical and mental health needs. UMSI students are encouraged to prioritize their health and take advantage of these resources throughout their academic journey.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

The University of Michigan's Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) provides mental health support through individual counseling, group therapy, and workshops. CAPS helps students manage challenges they may face during their academic career. The services are confidential and available at no extra cost to students.

Visit CAPS at U-M

Student Life and Organizations

UMSI strives to create a vibrant and diverse student life experience. There are various student organizations that support both professional and personal development. If you're interested in technology, social justice, or social events, there's a group for you. Students are encouraged to get involved to build connections, enhance their skills, and contribute to the community.

DEI Organizations

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) are core values at UMSI. There are numerous DEI-focused student organizations that work to promote inclusivity, understanding, and equal opportunities for all students. These organizations provide platforms for underrepresented groups and contribute to creating a welcoming and supportive environment for everyone in the UMSI community.
